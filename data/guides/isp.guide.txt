Setup Guide for iSmart Premuim
If you are seeing this and you have not used FlashcartHelper to help set up your iSmart Premuim, disregard this information.
This guide was made by ron975.


1)Drag everything in the "Put_In_SD_Card" (it should be open right now) into the ROOT of your MicroSD card.
2)Games and Homebrew can be anywhere you want in your MicroSD unless specified
3)Put your MicroSD card into your iSmart Premuim
4)Insert your iSmart Premuim into your DSi/DSlite/DSPhat
5)When you start your iSmart Premuim, a menu filebrowser should show, choose your game
6)Run "ISMART_firmware 4.nds" to upgrade your iSmart Premuim's firmware. THIS IS OPTIONAL. Also, Make sure your DS is plugged in if you decied to do this.
7)Enjoy!

Note: You cannot download commercial GBA/SNES/NDS roms through FlashcartHelper
