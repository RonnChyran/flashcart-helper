Setup Guide for iSmartMM
If you are seeing this and you have not used FlashcartHelper to help set up your iSmartMM, disregard this information.
This guide was made by ron975.

1)Drag everything in the "Put_In_SD_Card" folder (it should be open right now) into the ROOT of your MicroSD card.
2)Games and Homebrew can be anywhere you want in your MicroSD unless specified
3)Put your MicroSD card into your Supercard iSmartMM
4)Insert your iSmartMM into your DSi/DSlite/DSPhat
5)Start your iSmartMM
6)To play NDS files, start DS_Game from the menu you are at now
7)To play GBA games, start NDSGBA 


Note: You cannot download commercial GBA/SNES/NDS roms through FlashcartHelper
