Setup Guide for CycloDS Evoloution
If you are seeing this and you have not used FlashcartHelper to help set up your CycloDS Evoloution, disregard this information.
This guide was made by ron975.

1)Drag everything in the "Put_In_SD_Card" (it should be open right now) into the ROOT of your MicroSD card.
2)Games and Homebrew can be anywhere you want in your MicroSD unless specified
3)Put your MicroSD card into your CycloDS Evoloution
4)Insert your CycloDS Evoloution into your DSi/DSlite/DSPhat
5)When you boot your CycloDS, it asks you to update, choose yes.
6)Choose Game/Homebrew for Games and Homebrew, and Media for Moonshell

Note: You cannot download commercial GBA/SNES/NDS roms through FlashcartHelper
