Setup Guide for R4iDSN
If you are seeing this and you have not used FlashcartHelper to help set up your R4iDSN, disregard this information.
This guide was made by ron975.

1)Drag everything in the "Put_In_SD_Card" (it should be open right now) into the ROOT of your MicroSD card.
2)Games and Homebrew can be anywhere you want in your MicroSD unless specified
3)Put your MicroSD card into your R4iDSN
4)Insert your R4iDSN into your DSi/DSlite/DSPhat
5)When you start your R4iDSN, a menu should show. Choose "MicroSD card".
6)Enjoy!

Note: You cannot download commercial GBA/SNES/NDS roms through FlashcartHelper
